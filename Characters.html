Characters

You will play as one of the siblings.

Do you want to be the older sibling, who is protective, mature, grounded, but constantly distanting from the family?

Or do you want to be the younger sibling, who is rebellious, emotional, and constantly trying to get the attention of the older sibling, but is also constantly being ignored?

Characters

Default sibling pair: Older Sister & Younger Brother The player will become the older sister, while their sibling will be set as the younger brother.

Experience diverse growth and relationship dynamics. As a quite common pair, it is surely highly relatable to many players.

The game explores themes of family, emotional distance, growing up, favoritism, misunderstanding, and the realization that love does not always prevent separation.

Main Characters

Older Sibling

Protective, mature, grounded, but constantly distanting from the family.

Younger Sibling

Juvenile, curious, sensitive,and attention-seeking.

Character Background

Default Player-Character: An ordinary school girl(older sibling) with a younger brother, struggles to keep the “right distance“ throughout adolescence

Common pairs: An ordinary school boy(older sibling) with a younger brother

Age gap: 2 years

Conflict: Torn between social expectations, peer pressure, and sibling bonds Event 1: Car accident before the story starts Event 2: A fight over a gift from parents, a feeling of betrayal that never occured since the car accident, “stone“ gets thrown away, friendship ends

Choices: Whether or not to respond to younger brother's seeking of attention and whether or not to “share“ parental attention with younger brother
