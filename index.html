A decision-based story about two siblings growing together, drifting apart, and everything left unsaid in between.

Game Introduction

Close Apart is a decision-based game focused on the emotional development of two siblings, gender and order by player's choice.

Through arguments, quiet conversations, memories, jealousy, and reconciliation, players shape the relationship between the siblings over the course of three stages.

Some choices appear small. Some choices are unforgettable.

The game explores themes of family, emotional distance, growing up, favoritism, misunderstanding, and the realization that love does not always prevent separation.

The Teddy Bear

The teddy bear is one of the most emotional elements in the game. It means a lot to you character and your sibling. As the story progresses, the teddy bear's condition changes, too, reflecting the bond between you.


It's more than just a teddy bear; it's the connection between you; if you lose it, you lose a part of yourself, and it would be no more possible to mend your relationship than it is to fix a broken thread of string...
